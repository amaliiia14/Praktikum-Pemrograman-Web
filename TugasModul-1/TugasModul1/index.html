Vegetable's Salad

A vegetable platter is a dish consisting of a variety of raw or lightly cooked vegetables, usually served as an appetizer or snack. Common veggies include carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and broccoli. It is often accompanied by a dipping sauce like hummus or ranch.

Fruit's Platter

A fruit platter is a colorful assortment of fresh, cut fruits arranged on a plate. Popular fruits include watermelon, pineapple, strawberries, grapes, and berries. It's usually served at gatherings, parties, or as a healthy dessert option.

Salmon Pan Seared

Pan-seared salmon is a method of cooking salmon where the fillet is seared in a hot pan with oil or butter. This technique gives the salmon a crispy, golden-brown crust on the outside while keeping the inside tender and moist. It's often seasoned with herbs, salt, and pepper, and sometimes served with a lemon wedge or sauce.

Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is a popular dish made by spreading mashed or sliced avocado on toasted bread. It's often topped with a sprinkle of salt, pepper, chili flakes, or other seasonings, and sometimes additional toppings like a poached egg, feta cheese, or cherry tomatoes. It's a healthy, simple breakfast or snack.
